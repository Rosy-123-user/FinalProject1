About E-Shuri

E-Shuri is designed to provide students and teachers with all the resources they need for a successful learning experience. Explore modules, access resources, participate in discussions, and much more. E-Shuri was created by Roseline Niyigaba, who is passionate about improving educational experiences through technology.

Platform Features

  • Augmented Reality (AR) for interactive learning
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) for personalized education
  • Comprehensive English courses
  • Detailed grade tracking and reporting
  • Analytics and data generation for the Ministry of Education...etc
